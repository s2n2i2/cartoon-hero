Katara of the Southern Water Tribe

About Katara

Katara is a underrated character in Avatar: The Last Airbender. Although she may seem uptight, her determined, goal-oriented personality keeps the whole Avatar Gang in check.

Katara and the Avatar Gang

Katara's Characteristics

  1. Hair loops
  2. The love of Aang's life
  3. Basically the mom of the group

Katara's friends

Every member of the Avatar Gang has a crucial task on their mission. Aang, the Avatar, has to train so that he can reach his full potential. Katara keeps everyone on track. Without her, the rest of the gang would surely goof off. Sokka may be the comedic relief of the group, but he is so much more. He has a warm heart and he knows just how to cheer everyone up.
