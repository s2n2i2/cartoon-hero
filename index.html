Katara of the Southern Water Tribe

About Katara

Katara is a underrated character in Avatar: The Last Airbender. Although she may seem uptight, her determined, goal-oriented personality keeps the whole Avatar Gang in check.

Katara and the Gang

Katara's Characteristics

  1. Iconic hair loops
  2. The love of Aang's life
  3. The "mom friend"

Katara's friends

Every member of the Avatar Gang has a crucial task on their mission. Aang, the Avatar, has to train so that he can reach his full potential. Katara keeps everyone on track. Without her, the rest of the gang would surely goof off. Sokka serves as the comedic relief of the group. He knows how to get everyone in the right spirits. Toph is the muscle of the group.
